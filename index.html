BAWK!

EECS 493 Final Project

Hayden Spitzley, Matthew Cloutier,
Ali Abdulhamid, Kaitlyn Frank,
Daniel Miller


Our final project is BAWK!, a modern Android version of the 1990â€™s
puzzle game Zoop. BAWK! is a single-player application, and the player
takes on the role of a chicken defending itself from intruding multi-colored
eggs. The chicken can shoot an infinite amount of lasers out of its eyes,
which it uses against the eggs. If the chicken shoots an egg of a different
color, then the chicken and the egg swap colors. Otherwise, if they are
both the same color, the laser destroys the egg. The goal of the game is
to prevent the from reaching the nest, and the player earns a point
every time an egg is wiped out.
