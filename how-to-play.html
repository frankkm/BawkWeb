How to Play
















After hitting the play button from the home screen, the user is presented
with this screen, where they must choose a game mode. The game has two
variations: classic mode and puzzle mode. In classic mode, eggs generate
as time progresses. In puzzle mode, eggs only generate every time the
player moves the chicken. Besides this key difference, gameplay for each
mode is the same. The actual game begins after the player selects a
game mode.
















The game starts off with the chicken in the center of the nest,
and the surrounding farm is almost completely empty. The player
can move the chicken anywhere inside of the nest.













The chicken shoots a laser out of its eyes when the player taps the screen.
The laser corresponds to the color of the chicken. A laser destroys an egg if
the chicken is the same color as the egg. The player receives a point every
time an egg is destroyed.
















If the chicken and the egg are different colors, then they swap colors.
















Throughout gameplay, eggs begin randomly spawning around the nest.
In classic mode, the rate of egg generation depends on the difficulty the
player sets before playing the game.
















In puzzle mode, an egg only generates every time the player moves the
character.
















If multiple eggs in the same row are the same color, and the chicken is also
the same color as the eggs, all those eggs will be destroyed when the
chicken fires a laser at them. Destroying multiple eggs at once gives the
user a bonus on their score.
















However, if the chicken destroys an egg, and the egg behind the destroyed
egg is of a different color, then the chicken will swap colors with this egg.
















The game ends when a row of eggs reaches the nest (i.e. if there is a row
of 4 eggs and a fifth egg in that row generates, the game ends). Immediately
after this scenario happens, the game over screen appears.
















The game over screen appears for a few seconds. Afterwards, the player is
shown both the final score of the game and the overall high score for that difficulty. The player can either return to the main menu or play again.
